Aim: Each of the blocks in the right box either has the character da4 (big), li4(to set up) or ri4(sun) as a part of their character. Some characters have exactly two of the aforementioned characters.

You need to recognize which characters are neighbors (characters having da4 as a component are neighbors and so on) and place in the grid on the left by clicking on them.

The scoreboard will update after each placement. Try to score above 75.
